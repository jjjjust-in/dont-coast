Tour Divide

The Tour Divide is a 2,700-mile self-supported mountain bike race from Banff, Alberta to Antelope Wells, New Mexico, following the spine of the Rocky Mountains along the Continental Divide. No crew, no support, no set schedule. Riders carry everything they need and navigate entirely on their own, moving through some of the most remote terrain in North America.

My Tour Divide Story

Fourth Tour Divide attempt in as many years. A month before my first Tour Divide attempt I lost my job. Sold my truck to fund the second. Maxed out credit cards on the third. This year will be no different. I'm not telling you this for your sympathy or to prove my stupidity. I'm telling you because there is no perfect scenario that leads you to the start line. There never will be. You go anyway. That's what this ride means to me. If it means something to you too, consider sending me a few dollars.
