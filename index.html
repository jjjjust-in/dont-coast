Tour Divide

June 12, 2026

A 2,700-mile self-supported mountain bike race from Banff, Alberta to Antelope Wells, New Mexico, following the spine of the Rocky Mountains along the Continental Divide. No crew, no support, no set schedule. Riders carry everything they need and navigate entirely on their own, moving through some of the most remote terrain in North America.
